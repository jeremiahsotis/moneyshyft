## Root Cause Themes

### Theme 1: Late hardening cycles

- Pattern: Initial implementation passed primary behavior checks, then review cycles exposed cross-cutting security and consistency gaps.
- Impact: Rework and review churn concentrated late in story lifecycle.

### Theme 2: Cross-cutting guardrails validated late

- Pattern: Auth, scope, idempotency, and durable-audit expectations were enforced in follow-up passes rather than at first implementation pass.
- Impact: Teams spent effort reconciling behavior after integration had already expanded.

### Theme 3: Critical dependency sequencing

- Pattern: Timezone canon story (`2.6`) remained at `ready-for-dev` while upcoming dispatcher planning relies on consistent time behavior.
- Impact: Elevated risk for Epic 3 planning and publish semantics unless timezone work is closed first.
