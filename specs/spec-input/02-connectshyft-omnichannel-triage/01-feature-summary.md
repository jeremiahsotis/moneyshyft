# ConnectShyft Omnichannel + Triage — Feature Summary

## What is being built

ConnectShyft will be upgraded into the primary omnichannel communications and intake hub for ShyftUnity.

This feature adds and formalizes support for:
- phone
- SMS
- MMS
- email
- webchat
- website contact forms / website forms

It also introduces a central triage model so inbound communications that are not yet attached to a case, program, or person can be received, reviewed, assigned, linked, or converted into work.

## Why now

After the MoneyShyft PWA, the sharpest operational pain is intake and communications triage.

Today, communications may arrive through multiple channels and require manual coordination. Staff need one place to:
- receive inbound communications
- review them
- decide what they belong to
- route them to the right person, case, or program
- create new work when needed

This work is prioritized ahead of service matching, screening, documents, and referrals because it is the front door of operations.

## Who uses this

Primary users:
- intake staff
- call center staff
- reception staff
- Vincentians
- case managers
- program managers

Secondary users:
- supervisors
- triage coordinators
- future coalition intake teams

## Success definition

A staff user should be able to:
1. receive inbound communication from any supported channel
2. view it in one intake/triage workspace
3. search or create the right person/household
4. attach it to an existing case or program, or create new intake/case work
5. notify or assign the right staff member or team
6. preserve channel-specific meaning without losing cross-channel consistency
